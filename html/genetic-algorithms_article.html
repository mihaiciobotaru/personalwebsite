This article contains all my projects related to Genetic Algorithms made during the second year of University. Genetic Algorithms have been one of my favorite subjects. I have used them to solve multi-modal functions. In the homework 3, I've created an algorithms that aims to solve Travelling Salesman Problem using genetic algorithms.
